Exam Dashboard

The exam dashboard lets you communicate useful information during an exam to students. Click on

  • the flag to start the countdown
  • the toilet to (un-)restrict the toilet access

Parameter:

You can initialize the exam dashboard with some custom features; you need to give a few parameters to the URL, however:

  • ?id=IN2227 sets the IN???? Module number for the course
  • ?time=90 sets the remaining time for this exam
For example: http://www2.in.tum.de/~petter/examdashboard?id=in0003&time=180

Warning: Do not change tabs on the browser, this pauses the countdown.

Warning: This is a beta version, use at your own risk

Important

  • Turn off all electronic devices and put them in your bag
  • Prepare your Passport/student ID
  • Do not use red/green pens
  • Do not use thin line width pens
  • You can hand in early during the first 75mins
  • Only one person at the WC at all times
